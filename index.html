About me

Hi, I'm Dashiell! I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Graduate Field of Genetics, Genomics, and Development at Cornell University.
I am in the lab of Dr. Amnon Koren in the Department of Molecular Biology and Genetics, where we study DNA replication timing in humans. My dissertation research focuses on uncovering heterogeneity in replication timing at the single-cell level, in order to better understand the regulation of this phenomenon.
